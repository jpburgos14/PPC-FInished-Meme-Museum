Definition of Memes

A meme is an idea, behavior, or style that spreads from person to person within a cultureâ€”often with the aim of conveying a particular phenomenon, theme, or meaning represented by the meme.

A meme acts as a unit for carrying cultural ideas, symbols, or practices, that can be transmitted from one mind to another through writing, speech, gestures, rituals, or other imitable phenomena with a mimicked theme. Supporters of the concept regard memes as cultural analogues to genes in that they self-replicate, mutate, and respond to selective pressures.

What are memes for Filipinos?

For Filipinos, memes are more than just funny images or videos; they are a form of social commentary, a way to connect with others, and a reflection of their unique cultural identity.

Filipino memes often incorporate local humor, languages, and pop culture references, creating a sense of community and shared experience. They can be used to express opinions, spark discussions, and even cope with challenges.
